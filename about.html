About Abu Zayd Institute

Our Vision. Our Mission. Our Method.

Abu Zayd Institute is an online Madrasah committed to nurturing spiritually strong, intellectually sound, and purpose-driven Muslims. We provide accessible Islamic and personal development education regardless of location, age, or background.

Many young Muslims today desire to learn Qur’an, Tajweed, Hadith, and Islamic values but face challenges such as limited access, unstructured learning, or lack of proper mentorship. That’s why we designed our programs to be flexible, practical, and inspiring — using platforms like WhatsApp, Zoom, and downloadable PDFs.

Our teaching goes beyond memorization. We empower students to *live* what they learn: praying properly, avoiding sins, dressing modestly, respecting parents, and building character according to the Qur’an and Sunnah.

Whether you’re just beginning your Qur’an journey, returning to Islam, or seeking the right mentorship — our team is here to support you with sincerity, structure, and smiles.

🌿 We currently offer training in:
Qur’an Memorization, Tajweed, Hadith, Adhkaar, Islamic Studies, and Personal Development.

Join a global community of youth and adults from Nigeria, the UK, the US, and beyond — becoming better Muslims, one class at a time.

With du‘ā and dedication,
— Abu Zayd Institute Team
